What information is collected?

BoxCheckr collects only the following information to verify your machine's security posture:

  • Computer name - Your machine's hostname
  • Operating system - OS type and version (e.g., macOS 14.0, Windows 11)
  • Disk encryption - Whether FileVault, BitLocker, or LUKS is enabled
  • Antivirus status - Whether XProtect, Windows Defender, or other AV is active

We do NOT collect: passwords, files, browsing history, keystrokes, screenshots, or any personal data.
